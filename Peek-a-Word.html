Peek-A-Word is a videogame app focused on helping kids to learn spanish grammar through differents types of minigames.

IDEA

Game with predetermined history and a series of minigames

The user advances in history and is unlocking mini-games. Raised and designed to reinforce and improve the child's prior knowledge of grammar and spelling.

Fun learning methodology, without the stress, pressure and rigidity of the classroom.

Target audience: boys and girls from 7 to 10 years old.

CONCEPT AND INSPIRATION

The design is based on the legend of the Atlantida.

It will create a planet that will resemble the characteristics of this fantastic territory.

DESIGN & MINIGAMES

Home

Each kid choose an username that will be the name of the main character through the app. In every minigame, the guide character refers to the main character with the username selected.

Menu

In the menu, kids can choose between the different minigames. Each one is focused to teach different spanish grammar areas such as punctuation, syllables or the classification of words.

MINIGAMES

Trivia

In this minigame, kids have to complete the sentence with the appropiate ortography opf the word. The user selects between the multiple options until he selects the right answer. The kid doesn't lose but gets a visual feedback when he fails.

Shooter

In this minigame, before it begins, it is show to the user wich group of words they have to tap. Then, kids have to tap all the creatures that have words that belong to the group showed before.Everytime he misses, he gets a feedback but doesn't lose the game.

Puzzle

In this minigame, the user gets different pieces of a puzzle that contains each one a syllable of a specific word. The user have to drag each piece in the correct order to complete a bridge that will allow the main character to go from one side to another. When the kid drag the incorrect piece, it returns to his original position allowing the kid to try as many times as he wants until he gets the right answer.

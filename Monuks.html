Monuks is an augmented reality mobile videogame for android and ios that is attached to an special target printed on the back cover of a notebook that have an special and uniques design.

NOTEBOOKS DESIGN

Back Cover

In the back cover of each notebook, we included the instructions on how to use the app combine with the notebook. Also, we put the augmented reality target in the center of the back cover so the users could focus their phone's camera and play the videogame.
